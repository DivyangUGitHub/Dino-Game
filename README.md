# Webcam Dino Game

A fun and interactive game where you control a dinosaur by moving your body in front of a webcam. Jump and duck to avoid obstacles, just like the classic Chrome Dino game but with motion control!

## Features

- **Motion Control**: Use your body movements to control the dinosaur
- **Real-time Webcam Detection**: See yourself on screen with motion tracking overlay
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Game speeds up as your score increases
- **Multiple Obstacles**: Avoid cacti, birds, and rocks
- **Score Tracking**: Local high score saving
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and tablet devices

## How to Play

1. Allow camera access when prompted
2. Stand about 2 meters from your webcam
3. Click "Calibrate" to set up motion detection
4. Click "Start Game" to begin
5. **Jump** in real life to make the dinosaur jump
6. **Duck/Crouch** to make the dinosaur duck
7. Avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible!
